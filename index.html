Welcome to a classic word - guessing game that offers two modes for players: Easy and Hard.

Easy mode

In the easy mode, the player must guess a letter to locate it within the predetermined word. Correct guesses will reveal the exact positions of the letters in the word.

Hard mode

In the hard mode of the game, player are required to click on an empty symbol, which represents a blank space in the word. Afterward, they need to guess the letter that corresponds to that symbol. If their guess is correct, the letter will be revealed in its respective positions in the word.

Goal

The objective in both game modes is to guess the complete word correctly. An incorrect letter guess will lead to a decrement in the guesses counter, and if the counter reaches zero, the game is lost.
